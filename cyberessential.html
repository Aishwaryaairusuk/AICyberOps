What is Cyber Essentials?

Cyber Essentials is a UK government-backed certification scheme designed to help organisations protect themselves from common cyber threats. It provides a clear framework of five key security controls that reduce the risk of cyber attacks by up to 80%.

The Five Key Controls

  • Use of firewalls and secure internet gateways
  • Secure configuration of systems and devices
  • User access control management
  • Protection against malware and viruses
  • Regular patch management and software updates

Why Get Certified?

- Demonstrate your organisation’s commitment to cybersecurity.
- Meet government contract requirements.
- Protect customer data and enhance trust.
- Gain a competitive edge in your industry.
